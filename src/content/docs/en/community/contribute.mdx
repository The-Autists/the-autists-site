# Contributions to The Autists are complicated

### in order for you to contribute:
 - You must have initmate knowledge of the Rust Programming Language
 - You must have a knowledge of the way that Drivers and SDK works
 - You must either be approved by an The Autists or a [helctic](https://helctic.dev) Core Dev

 ## Most of the Discussion happens in our [Discord](/en/community/discord) You can read more on the linked page
